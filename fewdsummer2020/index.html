Tuition and Benefits

We respect the financial commitment that families make when they choose to enroll their children in a online school. We've worked hard to offer the highest-caliber education at a remarkable value. As a result, tuition is significantly less than typical online schools. We can also work with you on a payment plan to fit your financial needs. Plus, we offer sibling discounts, so you can save 10% on tuition for each subsequent sibling you enroll as a full-time student. Learn More!

Family/Sibling Discounts

When two or more children in the same family are enrolled in S.T.E.A.M. Academy at the same time. You can save 10% on tuition for each subsequent sibling you enroll as a full-time student. ​
If a family has been receiving the discount, then later finds itself with only one student who remains enrolled in S.T.E.A.M. Academy for any reason (such as when a sibling withdraws from the program, transfers to another school, or graduates from S.T.E.A.M Academy), the family will not be eligible for the discount the next time the remaining student enrolls in a course.

Tuition Refund Plan

Why You Need The Tuition Refund Plan

Your financial obligation to the school is for the full tuition as stated in the school's enrollment contract. The school can not refund or cancel obligations is your child is forced to withdraw during the academic year.

If your son or daughter withdraws, the Tuiuion plan will pay benefits to the school, which provides substancial assistance in meeting your financial obligation.

Every year, hundreds of students must withdraw from schools. The following are examples why:

  • Family Move
  • Injury or Sickness
  • Death of Parent or Student
  • Disciplinary Dismissal
  • Chamghe of Objective
  • Scholastic Difficulties
  • financial Problems
  • Mental Health Conditions
  • Loss of Job
